I'm Mel ,

and as you can see above, we have selfie culture at it's finest. I really just wanted to smile at you since you've visited my site. #obligatorySultriness. I started working with metal when I was still in the womb and I've never stopped. Just kidding. I took a class, it's a thing you do. But then studio time and classes became too expensive. And then I randomly met a dude who helped me set up shop in my tiny studio apartment. He's kind of a pain in the ass, but I'm up and running on my own now! #mySoulIsMadeOfYay. If you have any questions, please don't hestitate to ask! I love working with people to craft something that is unique and exactly to the measure of their imagination. I often think about how we have always adorned ourselves with metals since culture has begun, and metal, unlike so many other things that we buy and share and gift, well, it endures. The idea that I might make something for someone that could become a family heirloom just boggles my mind. I'm honored to do this work, so drop me a line, I'm sure we can knock together something that will delight.
