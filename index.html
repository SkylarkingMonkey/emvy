Custom Jewelry and Metalwork

EMVY Jewelry is dedicated to handcrafted, custom jewelry for those seeking for unique pieces that cannot be found anywhere else.

Every piece from EMVY is created and crafted by Melanie (who probably has a last name that starts with a V). Currently stationed in Vancouver, pieces are available for shipment anywhere in the world. Be sure to check out @slimmelk on instagram where you can see ENVY jewelry and the long and laborious processes required to bring these beautiful pieces to fruition.


This website is just coming into being, and it is a work in process.
